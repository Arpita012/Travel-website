Charminar

The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is famous for its intricate architecture, which makes it one of the most recognized structures of India. Charminar was built to acknowledge the elimination of the plague which had affected the entire city immensely during that era. It is believed that the Sultan had prayed for the end of this plague with which his people were suffering.
